About

FRØST crafts timepieces inspired by the raw beauty of Nordic lands — where ancient myths meet modern design.

Born from the stillness of Nordic forests and the power of Viking steel, we create watches where time is respected, and design is meaningful. We are a tribute to the North, guided by Scandinavian principles: clarity over clutter, function over fashion, authenticity over flash.

Inspired by frost-covered landscapes and ancient Norse craftsmanship, each piece captures nature’s soul and heritage’s strength — bold yet minimal, sustainable, and striking like runes on stone.

To wear FRØST is to carry the North on your wrist: elegance carved from ice, time told simply.
